InvoiceFlow

A production SaaS platform that turns invoice photos into structured data at 95% accuracy — with 8 AI agents handling everything from OCR to billing.

The Problem

Small businesses spend hours every week manually entering invoice data. They photograph or scan invoices, then retype every field — supplier name, amounts, dates, line items — into their accounting software. Mistakes are common, and the process does not scale.

Existing OCR solutions either lack accuracy on messy real-world invoices or charge prohibitive per-page fees. Most small businesses cannot justify enterprise OCR pricing for their volume.

We needed an end-to-end solution: snap a photo, get structured data, track payments, and manage the entire invoice lifecycle — all from one platform with SaaS-grade reliability.

Our Solution

InvoiceFlow is a complete SaaS platform built as a Turborepo monorepo with a React frontend, FastAPI backend, and PostgreSQL database. The core innovation is a Gemini-powered OCR pipeline that achieves 95%+ accuracy on real-world invoice photos — including crumpled receipts, angled shots, and multi-language documents.

Eight AI agents handle different aspects of the workflow: document classification, field extraction, data validation, duplicate detection, currency conversion, payment tracking, anomaly flagging, and report generation. The agents work as a pipeline — each one refines the data before passing it forward.

Stripe handles subscription billing with multiple tiers, and the entire system is GDPR compliant with proper data retention policies and user consent management.

Tech Stack

Key Features

95%+ OCR Accuracy

Gemini-powered extraction that handles real-world conditions: poor lighting, angles, crumpled paper, handwritten notes, multiple languages.

8-Agent Pipeline

Classification, extraction, validation, dedup, currency, payments, anomaly detection, and reporting — each agent specializes in one task.

Turborepo Monorepo

Shared packages across frontend, backend, and AI services. Type-safe end-to-end with shared interfaces and validation schemas.

Stripe Billing

Multiple subscription tiers, usage-based pricing for OCR calls, automatic invoice generation, and payment retry logic.

GDPR Compliance

Data retention policies, user consent management, export/delete functionality, and audit logging for regulatory compliance.

3D Landing Page

Interactive Three.js landing page with animated invoice models, smooth scroll transitions, and conversion-optimized design.
