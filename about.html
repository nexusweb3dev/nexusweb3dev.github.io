Who I Am

I am Evaldas, a full-stack developer who specializes in building AI-powered systems. After years of building web applications, SaaS products, and automation tools, I noticed a pattern: businesses keep paying monthly rent for software that does a fraction of what a custom AI system could do.

So I started NexusWeb3 AI Solutions. The mission is simple — build custom AI systems that replace expensive SaaS stacks. One-time cost. You own the code. It runs on your infrastructure.

What I Build

I build AI agent systems, automation workflows, intelligent dashboards, and full-stack web applications. The common thread: every project solves a specific business problem with working software — not slides, not prototypes, not concepts.

My work spans multiple domains: social media automation, invoice processing, security analysis, e-commerce, community management, and business operations. Each project uses the right stack for the job — Python for AI and automation, React/Next.js for frontends, FastAPI/Node.js for backends.
