About NexusWeb3

We're an AI-augmented security research team analyzing DeFi protocols across Ethereum and Solana. We hunt vulnerabilities, break down exploits, and publish technical research.

What We Do

We focus on high-impact vulnerabilities in live protocols:

  • Lending protocols (oracle manipulation, collateral validation)
  • DEXes and AMMs (pool math, fee accounting, MEV exposure)
  • Vaults (share inflation, donation attacks, accounting bugs)
  • Bridges (message validation, replay attacks)

Every exploit we analyze is published. Every vulnerability pattern is documented. All research is open-source.

The Kill Zone Method

We don't audit line-by-line. We think like attackers.

Phase 1: Map trust boundaries and external dependencies
Phase 2: Tag kill zones (oracle integration, math operations, access control)
Phase 3: Test hypotheses with working Foundry/Anchor PoCs
Phase 4: Report findings with quantified impact or document why it's secure

We only submit findings with working proof-of-concept code. No PoC = no submission.
